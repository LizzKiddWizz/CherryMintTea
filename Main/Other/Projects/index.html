Long Term Projects

Projects that require a lot of development time, either from programming, writing, or worldbuilding.

Crucifiction

Gothic Strategy Relationship Sim Game On Hold

A strategy game built around managing the powers and struggles of infernal beings. You follow Kane or Abelia, a young adult barely scrapping by in a hellish city, recently annointed as the owner of a cursed grimoire. It sucks them into a corrupted scene and foists a responsibility upon them to soothe the tattered mind the landscape represents. With a gothic aesthetic with hints of religious imagery you trudge through a story focusing on finding faith in humanity and unraveling a plot against the very framework of the universe.

Stage: Concept

Gods Bleed

Cozy Cooking Sim Relationship Sim Game On Hold

This game centers around cooking and restaurant management. As well as building a relationship with your clients through finding out their preferred meals via conversation and advice. The main character is Beretta, a retiree whose children have grown and left her, dispersing throughout the world and its vast continents. She finally has achieved one of her final dreams. A restaurant. She has done everything in her power to attain this goal of hers, sometimes selfishly and in ways she feels guilty about. In order to settle her heart (and gain some courage), she does her best to redeem herself through good deeds and even greater food.

Stage: Concept

Meltdown

Sci-fi High Fantasy Grim Series Multiple Genres In Progress

A basket, within a basket, within a basket, within a basket. Near infinity. That is what it means to be an Althalmus. To manage the sheer exxpanse of the multiverse, beyond your sense of self, as it is your duty. Dante does not find this "duty" all that palattable. As he it is not as if he chose this fate that befell him. He simply died and was thrust into a position he cannot decline. You help him navigate his training, meet interesting people, and face concepts unique to the environment that is oh-so new to him. Explore. Document. Assist. Belittle. Love. Destroy. It is all up to you.

Stage: Concept

Side Projects

Works that I use to take a break from short-term and long-term projects.

Corposant

Court Intrigue Romance Strategy Relationship Sim Game Working

You are a young woman who has had their debutante in high society nearly half a year ago. It was merely a formality for you, as you are already well-known amongst the nobles. Your engagement had been decreed by the King himself, and was decided upon before you were even born. Since you are of age now, you are expected to marry soon and assist in the household matters of your betrothed, and eventually bear several children. However, you have not met the man who will become your husband. All you know, is that he is of high status worthy of your own, and that he is close in age to yourself. This is a management and romance simulator where you attempt to rebuild your estate while nurturing your bond with your new husband.

Stage: Concept
