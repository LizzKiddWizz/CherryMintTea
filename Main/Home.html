About Me

Hiyah! My name is Lizz, also known as LizzKiddWizz. I am an artist, a writer, and a soon to be developer. My goal when creating is summarized by three words, Connection, Compassion, and Continuity. I want to create connections, to concepts, to realizations, and most importantly, to people. I want to promote compassion, to characters, to ideas, and to community. And lastly, I want to maintain continuity, in myself, the depictions of the characters and what they represent, as well as in the environment the work exists in.


Though I believe that first and foremost, a book, a game, a movie, a story, is meant to entertain, that it should also leave you with something to think about. To improve upon, whether it be yourself, your relationships, or your world, however big or small it is. Because that is how it sticks with you. I'd argue that much of what you remember from your formative years are the people, and the content that you consumed. I know it shaped me, and likely shaped you as well. And that is part of my intent, to leave an impact, and make something someone can remember fondly.
